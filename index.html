FAQ

Answers to the questions clients usually ask first.

What kinds of items can I send?

Most hard goods, accessories, decor pieces, and structured wearables are great candidates. Each project is reviewed first to make sure the material and shape are a good fit.

Do you provide the item or do I send my own?

Most clients send their own item so the exact brand, fit, size, and style are already right. If sourcing is needed for a special project, that can be discussed during the quote process.

How long does it take?

Turnaround depends on the project size and current schedule. If you have a deadline, include it in the quote form so timing can be reviewed up front.

Can bedazzled items be washed or used heavily?

These are specialty custom pieces. They are best for events, gifting, display, careful handling, and light wear rather than machine washing, soaking, or rough daily use.
