FAQ

The questions people ask before they trust us with their favorite object.

What kinds of items can I send?

Most hard goods, accessories, decor pieces, and structured wearables work great. We review each item first to make sure the surface, shape, and use case are a good fit.

How long does it take?

Standard turnaround depends on the piece and current queue, but most customs land in the one-to-three week range after your item arrives. Rush options are available when capacity allows.

Do you provide the item or do I send my own?

Most clients send their own item so the fit, style, and brand are exactly what they want. For select projects, sourcing can be discussed during the quote stage.

Is it washable or waterproof?

Bedazzled pieces should be treated as specialty items. They are best for display, events, gifting, light wear, and careful handling rather than machine washing or soaking.
